Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

PlayDen is a free, community-focused gaming site run by a single person. This policy is written to be straightforward and honest, not to obscure anything. If you have questions, just email us.

Who We Are

PlayDen is a free browser-based gaming platform. It is operated independently by one person. You can reach us via the contact info on the About page.

What Data We Collect

When you create an account and play games, we collect and store the following:

  • Account information — your email address and the username you choose at sign-up.
  • Game data — your best scores and completion times for each game on the site.
  • Chip balance — your in-game chip total and the date of your last daily login bonus.
  • Poker statistics — wins and games played (for Texas Hold'em only).
  • Account creation timestamp — when your account was created.

We do not collect your real name, address, phone number, payment information, or any other personal details. You can play anonymously without an account, but scores and chips will not be saved.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate PlayDen:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account session.
  • To display your scores on public leaderboards (your username is shown, not your email).
  • To track and award your chip balance and daily login bonuses.
  • To let you view your game history and stats on your account page.

We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not use your data for advertising targeting. We do not share it with third parties beyond the services listed below that are required to run the site.

Third-Party Services

PlayDen relies on the following external services to function:

  • Firebase (Google) — used for user authentication and as the database that stores your account, scores, and chips. Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Your data is stored in Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Render.com — the poker game server is hosted on Render. No personal data beyond your user ID is processed by the server.
  • Google AdSense — PlayDen may display ads to help cover operating costs. Google uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings. Ad revenue goes entirely toward server and hosting costs.

Cookies & Local Storage

Firebase Authentication stores a session token in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in between visits. This token is used only to verify your identity with Firebase and is cleared when you log out. We do not use tracking cookies of our own.

If Google AdSense is active, it may set advertising cookies in your browser. You can manage these through your browser settings or the Google Ad Settings link above.

Leaderboards & Public Data

Your username and best scores are displayed publicly on the PlayDen leaderboard. Your email address is never shown publicly. If you would like your scores removed, contact us and we will delete them.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us (see the About page) and we will complete the deletion within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

PlayDen is a general audience site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Since PlayDen is a small, single-person project, major changes are unlikely — but we will always be transparent if they happen.

Contact

Questions about this policy or requests to access or delete your data:

See the About page for contact options.
